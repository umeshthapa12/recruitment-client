About Jobharu

Does jobharu guarantees us with Jobs?

jobharu is an online job search portal in Nepal. We only act as mediator between jobseekers and employer. If you meet all the requirements for any job you will have to apply for the jobs yourself. If you are shortlisted for the applied post the hiring company will contact you for further process.

Types of Vacancy

What do you mean by Top Job Service & Hot Job service?

What is category Jobs?

Job category is a section at jobharu that classifies the jobs according to various categories. Some of the categories may be accounting, human resource, banking, administration. This section is created to simplify the job search and posting for both the job seekers & the employers. Through job category, candidates can search for jobs according to their field of interest. This section can be seen at the bottom of the featured jobs.
